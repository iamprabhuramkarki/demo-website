BIT(Hons)

The need for qualified IT experts is necessary since the world becomes more sophisticated every day. You will be on the road to success and have outstanding work opportunities if you earn a bachelor's degree in information technology. Padmashree University's Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) program is connected with Nilai University in Malaysia. The goal of the information technology curriculum is to introduce students to programming as well as the foundations of computer networking, applications, and support. Blockchain and cutting-edge, contemporary courses on artificial intelligence are part of the degree program. In reality, the introduction of blockchain technology in Nepal began at Padmashree College.

BCA

The following positions in IT companies are open to BCA graduates: system analysts, managers, project managers, database administrators, designers, and programmers. Businesses in the manufacturing and information sectors are always looking for BCA graduates. A bachelor's degree in computer applications qualifies students to enroll in any of the MCA, MIT, MBA, and other programs provided by the School of Management and Information Technology.

B.TECH(Food)

The study of food from beginning to end, from the choice and harvesting of raw materials, to their composition and development, production, quality assurance, preservation and safety, packaging and storage, to the end-user preparation, consumption, and sensory evaluation. Food technology is the application of food science to the choice, preservation, processing, packaging, distribution, and consumption of wholesome, nutritious food. Due to its novelty as a field of study and the rising demand for food technologists in today's health-conscious society, food technology courses draw a sizable number of students.

While completing your bachelor's degree in hospitality management, you can gain practical experience in the field and graduate with the abilities you need to begin a new employment.
